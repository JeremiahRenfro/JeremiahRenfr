Millikin Womans Basketball first started in 1970 with only a few games to start their first start of their season. The past they were a decent basketball team and had competed for many titles throughout the time they have been playing basketball. This is defiantly a competitive team and not just a group of students who play basketball but a culture like sense and they love the game that they do their absolute best on the court.

This season they are competing for the national title for D3.
