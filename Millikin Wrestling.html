HISTORY

Millikin wrestling on the Millikin university website has much data as from 2015. So, we are going to talk about them from 2015-today. But they have NCAA appearances records that show that Millikin wrestling has been around since 1966 and they have been making appearances ever since then for numerous years. They have a lot of records from the past as well as today. The program was still growing when I was a freshman as I can remember but when they started, they started big and got some big wins and conference titles. They have built the foundation to where they are now.
